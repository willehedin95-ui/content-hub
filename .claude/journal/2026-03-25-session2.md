## Decisions made
- **`fixMetaImageUrls()` as post-optimization step**: Image optimizer does global URL→path replacement which breaks absolute URLs in meta tags. Rather than making the optimizer aware of meta tags, we fix them afterward. Simpler and less fragile.
- **Anatomy rules in prompt, not post-generation validation**: Cheaper to prevent bad images via prompt engineering than to generate + reject + retry. Added 6 explicit rules for body positioning.
- **Prefer behind-the-person shots**: Added to prompt rules — avoids face distortion which is the most common AI image tell.
