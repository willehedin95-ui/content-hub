## Decisions made
- **Sync-then-push cron architecture**: Single daily cron does metrics sync first (which may auto-kill and free slots), then pushes queued concepts. Order matters — frees slots before filling them.
- **Non-blocking Meta pausing**: Ad set pausing uses `Promise.allSettled` and logs errors but never blocks the kill operation.
- **Stage transition thresholds**: TESTING_DAYS=7, KILL_CPA_MULTIPLIER=2, SCALE_CONSECUTIVE_DAYS=5, SCALE_MIN_CONVERSIONS=3
