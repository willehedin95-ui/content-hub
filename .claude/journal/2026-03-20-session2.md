## Decisions made
- **No DB migration** — grouping happens at API/UI layer from existing `page_tests` rows (one per concept). Zero risk.
- **Winner declaration warns about outliers** — if a concept performs better on the losing page, the confirm dialog warns the user. After declaring, they can update that concept's landing page and re-push. Simpler than per-concept exception checkboxes.
- **Per-concept breakdown** shows ROAS, CPA, CVR per concept with "Favors A/B" indicator — lets user spot when specific concept angles perform better on a specific page (e.g. neck pain concept → advertorial, even if listicle wins overall)
