## Decisions made
- Image selection defaults all images to selected — user deselects what they want to keep
- Max 3 concurrent image generations to avoid overwhelming the API
- `replaceVideoWithImage()` copies width/maxWidth styles from the video element
- GIF and animated WebP treated as video (not image) since they work as video sources
