## Decisions made
- **Bleeders get same cooldown as everyone**: User asked "shouldn't we give them more time?" — agreed that Meta's learning phase needs at least 4 days regardless of spend.
- **Always inject productAppearance**: Even if `include_product_reference: false`, the text goes in. Prevents hallucinated product appearances (e.g. "dark amber glass bottle" instead of white Hydro13 bottle).
- **Native ads CAN show product**: Changed from rigid "native = no product" to context-dependent "is the product visible?" — because native selfies often show someone holding the product.
