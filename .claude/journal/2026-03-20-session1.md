## Decisions made
- **Non-DCO over DCO**: Meta broke `asset_customization_rules` compatibility with DCO. Non-DCO + PAC is the correct approach for routing images to specific placements. Non-DCO also allows multiple ads per ad set (DCO limited to 1).
- **No publisher_platforms override**: Template ad sets have no platform restriction, so we pass targeting as-is. Meta defaults to Advantage+ placements including Instagram, even without a linked Instagram business account (uses page-backed IG).
- **One ad per image pair**: Each source image gets its own ad with labeled 4:5 + 9:16 variants. This replaces the old single DCO creative with all images.
