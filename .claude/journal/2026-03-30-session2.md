## Decisions made
- **Don't lower DK budget** — kill zombies instead. DK full-month ROAS (1.78x) is at breakeven. The problem is too many bad ad sets, not the market itself.
- **Bleeder threshold = 200 SEK** — Any ad set spending 200+ SEK in 7 days with zero purchases gets auto-killed. This is aggressive but justified: zero conversions at that spend level never recovers.
- **Testing cooldown 4 days** — Was 7 days, too conservative. 4 days gives enough data for Meta to optimize while catching bleeders faster.
