## Internal Linking Rules

- Every article links back to its pillar
- Every article links to 2-3 sibling articles in the same cluster
- Pillar articles link down to all their cluster articles
- Cross-topic articles (7, 16) bridge both pillars
- All "bäst i test" articles include CTA to product page (get-renew.com for Hydro13, HappySleep.se for pillows)
- Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here")

## Content Quality Checklist

For every article:
- [ ] At least 1 study/source cited with link
- [ ] FAQ section with 3+ questions (auto-generates FAQ schema)
- [ ] Featured image set in builder settings
- [ ] SEO title with target keyword + year/brand
- [ ] Meta description 130-160 chars
- [ ] Internal links to 2+ other blog articles
- [ ] Category set correctly
- [ ] CTA box linking to relevant product page
- [ ] Language reviewed for YMYL compliance (no medical claims without source)
- [ ] Collagen articles: EFSA-compliant language ("studies suggest", "designed to support", not "treats/cures")
